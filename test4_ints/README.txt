	clear & php -S localhots:8000 index.php

Usage and Procedure:
	1. Open browser
	2. Navigate to `http://localhost:8000/`
	3. Observe functional output of `test_strings.php`

Purpose:
	1. To portray string concatenation.
	2. To portray funciton calling.
	3. To ensure we understand the different ways integers work.
	4. To ensure we understand how the different operators work.

List of all arithemtic operators in php:
	+$a			Identity				Conversion of $a to int or float as appropriate.
	-$a			Negation				Opposite of $a.
	$a + $b		Addition				Sum of $a and $b.
	$a - $b		Subtraction			Difference of $a and $b.
	$a * $b		Multiplication		Product of $a and $b.
	$a / $b		Division				Quotient of $a and $b.
	$a % $b		Modulo				Remainder of $a divided by $b.
	$a ** $b		Exponentiation		Result of raising $a to the $b'th power. Introduced in PHP 5.6.
